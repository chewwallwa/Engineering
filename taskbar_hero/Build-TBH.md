# Sorcerer

- weapon:  `(65 - arcana)`                          || (65 - divine) or (65~80 high)
- sub-wp:   (15 - arcana)
- helmet:   (50 - imortal) | (50 - beyond)          || (65 - celestial)
- armor:   `(65~80 - high)`
- glooves:  (80 - imortal) | (50 - beyond)          || (65 - arcana) 
- boots:    (65~80 - high)
- amulet:  `(65 - imortal)`| Emerald (50 - arcana)  || (80 - beyond, divine, cosmic) or (65 - celestial)
- earing:  `(80 - imortal)`                         || (65 - celestial or cosmic) 
- ring:     (65 - imortal)                          || (80 - arcana, celestial) or (65 - beyond) 
- bracer:   (50 - imortal, arcana)                  || (80 - beyond, cosmic)

- weapon decorations: lightining (until 400%) area of effect (until 400%)
- armor decorations: any or nothing (cooldown if need to top up)
- accessory decorations: area of effect (until 400%), move speed (all free spaces)

# Ranger

- weapon:   (65 - arcana)                           || (65 - beyond) (80 - celestial or higher)
- sub-wp:  `(80 - imortal)`                         || (80 - arcana, beyond or higher) (65 - cosmic)
- helmet:   (65~80 - high)
- armor:    (65~80 - high)
- glooves: `(80 - imortal)`| (50 - beyond)          || (65 - arcana) 
- boots:    (65~80 - high)
- amulet:   (80 - imortal)                          || (65,80 - arcana) (65 - divine, cosmic) 
- earing:  `(80 - imortal)`                         || (65 - cosmic) 
- ring:     (65 - arcana)                           || (65 - celestial) 
- bracer:   (65 - arcana)                           || (65 - beyond, celestial) 

- weapon decorations: physical dmg or atk dmg % (you will have to calculate which combination will be stronger)
- armor decorations:  any or nothing, maybe dodges 
- accessory decorations: move speed (full if solo ranger. if party, try to equilibrate), crit chance
% don't use atk speed decorations if higher than 6 (cap)

- accessory decorations:  move speed (to equilibrate with party), HP and HP%
